Enrollment in our rapier school is completed via the SeaTac Community Center on a quarterly basis. Quarterly tuition is $45.

Our class is held on Wednesday evenings from 7pm to 9:30pm. The first hour is focused on stretching, strengthening, and drilling of fundamentals of footwork and bladework. The material of the classes is customized to the attendees, and new fencers receive one-on-one instruction from our teacher or an advanced student.

The remainder of the class is dedicated to sparring in which intermediate and advanced students learn and practice through full-speed high-intensity combat.

Class Options
  • Adult(18+) Beginning
  • Adult Intermediate
  • Adult Advanced
  • Youth(10~17) Beginning
  • Youth Intermediate
  • Youth Advanced

We have loaner equipment available for new members. As students advance and want to start participating in tournaments and events outside of the school, they can begin purchasing their own equipment.

The most important purchase is a sword. Castille Armory is a good choice for top-quality weapons. In addition, a standard 3-weapon fencing mask and gorget are required to spar.
