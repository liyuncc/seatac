Welcome to Seatac Rapier School

The SeaTac Rapier School teaches beginners through competitive-level fencers the art of historical rapier combat - the martial ancestor of today's olympic-style fencing. We are a local chapter of the Band of the Black Tigers, and participate in events of the Society for Creative Anachronism. New fencers and visitors from other schools are always welcome at our practice! We're looking forward to seeing you soon!
